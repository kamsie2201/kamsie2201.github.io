What Is a Traffic Bot?

A bot is an automated tool that can easily add hundreds to thousands of views to your website. With it, you can buy organic search traffic using keywords that you want to be associated with your site. The goal is for this traffic to help your website rank higher on search engine results pages using specific keywords.

The best part about bots is that they can provide unlimited keyword targeted traffic. You can pay for as little or as much traffic as you need to help your site achieve a higher rank. It's the perfect option for small businesses that want to compete with larger, more well-known businesses.

Traffic bots are different from services that use real people to click on your website. Instead of using bots, they pay real people, so they have to charge more to cover the overhead of having more employees. Also, you can only generate so many views with those services because real people can only click on so many links. On the other hand, bots can click on unlimited links.
