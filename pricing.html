Frequently Asked Questions

What is an action?

An action is what a visit can do: visit a page, click on a link, wait X seconds or fill a form. For example, if you want a launch to visit two pages and wait 45 seconds between, it will be 3 actions (action 1: visit a page, action 2: wait 45 seconds, action 3: visit another page).

How long does it take for my campaign to start after purchase?

Less than a minute. Your account is automatically credited after we receive your payment.

What if I exceed my plan's action limit?

If you configure more actions per day than your current plan, we apply a multiplying factor to all your campaigns so you stay inside the limit of your plan. We don't charge any extra for this

Can I upgrade or downgrade/switch plans at any time?

Yes. Babylon Traffic is a pay-as-you-go service and you can upgrade, downgrade or cancel at any time. Click on "My subscription" in your Babylon Traffic account to change your plan.

What is a campaign?

A campaign is the configuration of how the visits reach your websites website. It is the "behavior" (the path they follow) of the visits and the settings they have: geo-targeting, bounce rate, time on site, mobile visits, etc.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

There's no contract - just pay as you go, month-to-month. You are free to cancel whenever you need.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the main credit cards company: Visa and Mastercard and almost all the cryptocurrencies in the market. If you'd like us to pay with a wire, contact us!

Can I cancel my account at any time?

Yes. If you ever decide Babylon Traffic isn't the best tool for your business, simply cancel your account from your subscription page.
